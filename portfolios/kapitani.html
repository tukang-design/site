UI Revamp & Design System

Kapitani App: Farm Management

Project Overview

Kapitani App is a mobile application designed to help modern farmers manage their operations, from crop cycles to inventory. We were tasked with a complete UI revamp to improve usability and to establish a scalable design system for future development.

Services: UI/UX Design, Design System, Prototyping

The Challenge

The existing app was functional but suffered from an inconsistent and outdated user interface. Users reported difficulty navigating the app and finding key features. The lack of a design system meant that every new feature slowed down development and introduced more inconsistencies.

Our Process & Solution

Our process began with a deep dive into user feedback and a thorough audit of the existing app. We identified key pain points and areas for improvement. We then developed a new, modern visual language that was clean, accessible, and easy to understand for users of all technical skill levels.

The core of our solution was the creation of a comprehensive design system. This included a full component library, clear typography scales, a defined color palette, and usage guidelines. This system not only brought consistency to the app but also dramatically sped up the development team's workflow for future updates.

The Results

The revamped Kapitani App was met with positive feedback from users, who praised its ease of use and modern look. The development team reported a 40% reduction in time spent on building new features, thanks to the robust and well documented design system.
