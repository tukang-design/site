About Tukang Design

We believe great design is a business tool.

Why "Tukang"?

In Malaysia, a 'Tukang' is a craftsman. A skilled, hands on expert you trust to get the job done right. They do not just work; they build, they fix, they create with purpose. We chose this name because it perfectly represents our approach. We are not a typical "agency." We are digital craftsmen. We roll up our sleeves and work alongside you as a partner. We believe the best design is not just about looking good; it is about building something that works.

The Mark of a Craftsman

Our logo is more than just a symbol; it is a story. It is a modern interpretation of the Jawi letters 'Ta' (ت) and 'Dal' (د), which stand for 'Tukang Design'. This choice is a deliberate nod to our Malaysian heritage. In the spirit of inclusivity common in local businesses, the name also translates to Chinese (设计工匠) and Tamil (வடிவமைப்பு கைவினைஞர்), reflecting our commitment to serving all of Malaysia's communities. The mark itself represents the building blocks of digital creation, structured to form a solid foundation and reflecting our values of partnership and craftsmanship in every line.
